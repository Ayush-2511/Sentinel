# SENTINEL - Demo Script (3-Minute Pitch)

## 1. Opening Hook
*(Visual: Blank map slowly illuminating with disaster zones)*
"Every second counts in a disaster. But when communications fail, and data is fragmented, human commanders freeze. What if the responders themselves could talk, reason, and act in perfect unison? Welcome to SENTINEL."

## 2. What Problem SENTINEL Solves
"In large-scale emergencies, we face a triage crisis: too many victims, too few resources, and chaotic decision-making. SENTINEL is a Multi-Agent AI Disaster Response System that replaces chaos with calculated, real-time autonomous strategy."

## 3. Meet the Team (The 3 AI Agents)
"Instead of one overloaded AI, we built a specialist task force. Meet our agents:
- **CASPER (Medical):** Evaluates civilian health and prioritizes life-saving triage.
- **MELCHIOR (Logistics):** Tracks supply caches and optimizes delivery routes.
- **BALTHASAR (Rescue):** Deploys extraction units to collapsed structures and hazardous zones."

## 4. Live Demo Flow
- **Start:** Load the `earthquake.json` scenario.
- **Action:** Show the agents analyzing the 10x10 grid. Point out how Casper flags a critical civilian while Melchior simultaneously identifies the closest medical cache.
- **Conflict & Resolution:** Trigger a scenario where agents disagree (e.g., rescue vs. medical priority). Show the real-time voting and reasoning process as they autonomously agree on the best course of action.
- **Outcome:** The agents deploy the limited medical teams optimally, saving the most critical civilians.

## 5. Why This Wins the Hackathon
"SENTINEL isn't just a chatbot wrapper. We built a robust, locally executing multi-agent ecosystem. It demonstrates genuine autonomous collaboration, conflict resolution, and handles complex constraints in real-time."

## 6. Future Scalability
"Tomorrow, SENTINEL scales to real city grids using GIS data, drone feeds, and direct IoT integration with emergency response vehicles. We can easily add new agents like HAZMAT or FIRE."

## 7. Strong Closing Line
"When the next disaster strikes, SENTINEL won't just analyze the data. It will save lives. Thank you."
